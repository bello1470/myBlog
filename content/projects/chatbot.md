# Abstract 

The University IT Services Chatbot project is a groundbreaking solution developed as part of my final year Master's degree project in Computer Science at Swansea University. This project was motivated by the need to improve accessibility to IT services within the university, aiming to enhance the user experience for students, faculty, and staff by providing a reliable and efficient digital assistant.

The chatbot utilizes Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to understand and respond to user inquiries related to IT resources, support, and services. By integrating with the university's existing IT systems and databases, the chatbot delivers timely and accurate information, significantly reducing the response time for IT support requests.

Developed using Python and Flutter, the chatbot employs machine learning algorithms to continually refine its understanding and response capabilities, ensuring a personalized and effective interaction with users. The project involved collaboration with university stakeholders, including IT administrators and end-users, to ensure alignment with actual user needs and expectations.

The decision to focus on this project for my Master's degree was driven by a desire to explore the potential of AI-driven solutions in transforming educational environments.
